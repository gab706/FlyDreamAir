Cookies Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to improve user experience and website functionality.

2. Why We Use Cookies

FlyDreamAir uses cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions properly.
  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Improve the speed and security of the website.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior.
  • Display personalized content and ads.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g. login, navigation).
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze site traffic and improve user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your choices and preferences.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track browsing habits to deliver relevant ads via third-party services.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control or delete cookies at any time via your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may impact your ability to use certain parts of our site.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by trusted third-party services such as Google Analytics or social media integrations. We do not control these cookies and recommend checking the privacy policies of those services.

6. Consent

By continuing to use our website, you agree to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You may opt out or adjust your settings at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy periodically. The updated version will be posted on this page with the new effective date.
