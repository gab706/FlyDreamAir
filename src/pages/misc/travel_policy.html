Travel Policies

Effective Date: May 19, 2025

1. Booking Requirements

Passengers must ensure all information provided at the time of booking is accurate. Any discrepancies may result in denied boarding or additional fees.

2. Identification and Documentation

All travelers are required to carry valid government-issued photo identification. International travelers must possess valid passports and any applicable visas.

3. Baggage Policy

  • Carry-on baggage must not exceed size and weight limits specified on our website.
  • Checked baggage allowances vary by fare class and route. Excess baggage may incur fees.
  • Prohibited items include weapons, flammable substances, and certain electronics.

4. Check-In and Boarding

Passengers must check in at least 2 hours before departure for domestic flights and 3 hours for international flights. Boarding gates close 20 minutes before departure.

5. Flight Changes and Cancellations

Flight changes are subject to availability and may incur fees. Cancellations are handled per the fare rules selected during booking.

6. Travel Disruptions

In case of delays, cancellations, or other disruptions, FlyDreamAir will offer rebooking options or refunds in line with our service terms and local regulations.

7. Passenger Conduct

FlyDreamAir expects all passengers to conduct themselves respectfully. Unruly behavior may result in removal from the aircraft or a ban from future flights.

8. Contact and Assistance

For questions or special assistance, please contact our support team at support@flydreamair.com.
