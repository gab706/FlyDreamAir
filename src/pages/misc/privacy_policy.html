Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2025

1. Introduction

FlyDreamAir is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services.

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, payment details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process bookings and provide travel services.
  • To personalize your experience and deliver tailored offers.
  • To communicate updates, promotions, and important notices.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your data with trusted third parties, including payment processors, travel partners, and regulatory authorities, only when necessary for service delivery or compliance.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption and access controls. While we strive to secure your information, no method is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

  • Access or request a copy of your personal data.
  • Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion or restrict processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance site performance and personalize your experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Policy Updates

We may periodically update this policy. Changes will be posted on our website with the revised effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding this policy, contact us at privacy@flydreamair.com.
